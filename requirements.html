Grade-Specific Requirements

Elementary (K-5)

  • Age-appropriate developmental assessment
  • Previous school reports (if applicable)
  • Parent interview focusing on child's needs
  • Basic reading and math readiness evaluation

Middle School (6-8)

  • Minimum 2.5 GPA from previous school
  • Standardized test scores (if available)
  • Extracurricular activity participation
  • Character reference from previous teacher

High School (9-12)

  • Minimum 3.0 GPA from previous school
  • SAT/ACT prep test scores (grades 11-12)
  • Leadership experience or community service
  • Academic and character recommendations

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application fee?

The application fee is $50, which covers the cost of processing your application and is non-refundable.

Can I apply after the deadline?

Late applications may be considered on a case-by-case basis if space is available. However, priority is given to timely applications.

Is financial aid available?

Yes, we offer need-based financial assistance. Please complete the financial aid application along with your admission application.

What if my child has special needs?

We welcome students with diverse learning needs. Please indicate any special requirements in your application so we can ensure appropriate support.
